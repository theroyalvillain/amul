LEARN MORE ABOUT US

Founded in 1946, Amul has grown into a powerful testament to the power of farmer cooperation. We are deeply committed to sustainable practices, empowering rural livelihoods, and tirelessly delivering unadulterated, nutritious dairy products to every Indian household. Our successful journey is one of collective effort and shared success that spans over seven decades.
